    • Go'thentic - A Website for Authentic Travel Advice

    For this group project, our team created a website aimed at helping travellers craft an authentic travel experience. Our product focuses on creating a travellers' community where members can leave reviews of locations and specific activities, build and see recommended itineraries, and view culture tips for different destinations.

    Below, the main features of our website are shown through the high fidelity wireframes. Not shown are the prototype capabilities, which we created using Figma. We also performed user validation tests with our prototype to quantitatively and qualitatively determine the effectiveness of our design and make improvements based on feedback and pain points.

    Below is a lean canvas for the project, for which we identified the problem, solution, unique value proposition, and customer segment.

    We also created a User Story Map with a list of goals, activites, and tasks for the MVP and non-MVP products.
