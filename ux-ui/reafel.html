    • REAFEL - Heart Arrhythmia Tracking

    A group project where we designed a website and mobile app for tracking heart arrhythmias for patients wearing ECGs. Our mobile app provides patients with the ablitity to enter activities/symptoms that may correlate with the ECG data. That information is sent to their doctor who can view it through our website, and can then record result summaries through the site which become available to the user through their mobile app. Furthermore, our app allows users to easily share the data with those they choose. For this project we created a user journey map, lean canvas, wireframes, and a prototype. We performed user testing of two different versions of our prototype.
