    • Eventbox - A Community Platform for Students

    This was an entrepreneurship group project where we were creating an app to target university/college students. The app focuses around events, allowing students to create, browse, and attend student only events in their area. The main features are event browsing/posting and the ability to form groups based on common interests. Our team was responsible with creating the idea and business plan for our product, as well as conducting user research.
